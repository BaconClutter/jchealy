User Research and Personas

The hospital is an extremely dynamic and unpredictable environment, which makes it difficult to introduce new technology, especially when it alters daily tasks and workflows.

Contextual inquiry, shadowing, user testing, surveys and other techniques help us get an idea of what the real people, nurses, nurse unit administrators, patients etc. are doing and what they need.
